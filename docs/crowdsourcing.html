Submit a Climate Risk Report

Thank you for contributing to HCMC's climate resilience! Here's how you can share your observations:

  • Mark a location: Click on the map to pinpoint where you experienced the climate risk.
  • Select risk type:
    • Flooding (street flooding, drainage issues, water depth)
    • Heat stress (outdoor work exposure, inadequate cooling)
    • Air quality (pollution sources, visibility, health impacts)
    • Land subsidence (ground cracking, building tilt, infrastructure damage)
  • Provide details:
    • When did this occur? (date and time)
    • How severe was the impact? (1-5 scale)
    • Who was affected? (residents, workers, infrastructure)
    • What actions were taken?
  • Upload photos (optional, max 5MB) - Visual evidence helps planners understand local conditions.
  • Suggest solutions - What infrastructure or policy changes could reduce this risk?
